About

Women artists have been written out of art history for far too long.

Inspired by the recently published 'The Story of Art Without Men' by art historian Katy Hessel, this quiz will test your knowledge of the great women artists of yesterday and today.

The Rules of the Game

Each image has four options to choose from.
One option is the correct answer and other three are contemporaries of the artist or artists inspired by her work.
Select and option and click submit to see how well you know the Great Women Artists.

🎨 We hope you have fun and discover a lot about Great Women Artists 🎨
